The Dance Floor
A decade in Motion

Better Late
Than Never

On April 27th, 2016, we signed our marriage papers at a courthouse in Manhattan, NYC. It was a quiet Thursday morning with exactly one witness, Olli, and two other friends, Fede and Jacopo—whom we had met in a completely random way (ask us for the story over a glass of wine). We were (very!) young, and while uncertainty was all around us, we knew we had each other.

Since then, we have moved across continents, raised three chaotic children, and built a home away from home. Through every move and every milestone, we never forgot our promise to eventually throw the celebration we skipped. So, pack your bags! We are finally gathering the village to toast the ten-year journey that brought us here. Think of it as a long dinner party followed by a night on the dance floor that has been a decade in the making.

TL; DR

A decade of miles, moves, and three kids later, we are finally throwing the party that honors the journey and the people who walked it with us.

The Fine Print

Stuff you need to know.

wb_sunny

Beat the Heat

It’s July in Tuscany. We want you cool and comfortable. We're leaning into landscape colors: Sage, Sand, and White.

Men: Linen or cotton in light tones. We recommend no dark suits or tuxedos—you will regret it in the sun.

Women: Flowy dresses and breathable fabrics. DO NOT wear stilettos. The ground is grass. You will sink. We will laugh.
